Ralf

Mutual transformative engagement through programmable audio responsiveness

The Listening Loop: the system listens to the body through qualities and gestures. The dancer listens to the system through sound. Neither is in control. Both are responding. Every turn through the loop changes the next turn.

A composition has two dimensions. The Sonic World — melodies, rhythms, harmonics, sound design — is the composer's musical intent. The Tendencies — weights that shape how the world responds to movement — are the interaction design. Together they make a living composition.
