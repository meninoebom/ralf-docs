## The Why

Brandon is obsessed with **Afro-diasporic choreographic systems** — capoeira, bomba, vodun, samba, flamenco, the dance floor. These are systems of **mutually transformative exchange** between dance and music. They are self-organizing, generative, complex systems.

His fascination: what they mean, what they suggest as possibilities, and what happens if we start using the principles they embody as a **design grammar**.

RALF is an attempt to **operationalize that design grammar** using digital tools.

## The What

"RALF is infrastructure for creating real-time, mutually transformative dialogue between movement and music."

## Technologies of Remembering (the deeper framework)

Technology in the broad sense: **skillful means**. Transferable skillful means that people have developed since time immemorial to **remember that which is dismembered by conceptual and analytical capability.** Dance, music, ritual, ceremony — these are technologies. RALF is the next iteration, adapted to digital tools.

## The Anchor Sentence

> "I'm building infrastructure for the kind of mutually transformative exchange that has always existed in Afro-diasporic choreographic systems — and I need to move from building it to performing it."

## The Specific Ask (from Gray Area, but adaptable)
